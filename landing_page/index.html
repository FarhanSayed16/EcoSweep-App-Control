Advanced Hardware Architecture

A sophisticated integration of embedded systems, IoT processing, and multi-sensor navigation.

Mechanical & Processing Core

Powered by a dual-compute architecture bridging a Raspberry Pi 4 (high-level IoT & Bluetooth processing) and an Arduino Mega 2560 (real-time motor/sensor execution). Features a PCA9685-driven multi-DOF robotic arm for precise object manipulation, all powered by an optimized Li-Po/Li-Ion split-voltage management system.

Sensor Fusion Navigation Array

Equipped with a robust suite of environmental sensors including a 360-degree Ultrasonic & IR array, NEO-6M GPS, and a 6-axis MPU6050 IMU (Accelerometer/Gyroscope) coupled with an HMC5883L Magnetometer to achieve stable, semi-autonomous spatial awareness and terrain mapping.
